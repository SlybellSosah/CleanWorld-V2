# Keeping Your Home & Business Safe: Pest Control Service Guide (South Sudan)

This guide outlines our services in clear, simple language, focusing on how we help you protect your health, your property, and your business in South Sudan.

## 1. Mosquito & Malaria Protection

**The Goal**: We want to help you and your family stay safe from malaria, dengue, and other sicknesses spread by mosquitoes.

### What We Do:
- **Finding Where Mosquitoes Grow**: We look for and clear out standing water in yards, construction sites, and drainage areas where mosquitoes lay their eggs.
- **Protecting Inside Your Walls**: We use professional-grade treatments on your indoor walls that keep working for a long time to keep mosquitoes away.
- **Keeping Spaces Safe**: We use safe, effective products to keep your living and office areas free from mosquitoes.

**Our Approach**: We don't just spray randomly. We look at the weather and the season to make sure we are protecting you exactly when you need it most.

---

## 2. Protecting Against Termites & Insects

**The Goal**: Termites can destroy foundations and furniture, and cockroaches or ants can cause problems in kitchens and food areas. We are here to stop them.

### What We Do:
- **Protecting New Buildings**: We put down protective barriers in the ground before you pour your concrete floors to keep termites out forever.
- **Fixing Existing Problems**: If you have termites, we find the nest and remove it without needing to tear apart your walls or floors.
- **Keeping Kitchens Clean**: We set up clear rules and treatments for hotels, restaurants, and shops to keep cockroaches and ants away from your food.

**Our Approach**: We focus on sealing cracks and entry points so that insects cannot get inside in the first place, reducing the need for heavy chemical use later.

---

## 3. Keeping Pests Out of Your Buildings (Rodents)

**The Goal**: Rats and mice can damage your supplies and carry germs. We focus on keeping them out of your warehouses and buildings.

### What We Do:
- **Finding Weak Spots**: We inspect your building to see where pests are getting in (like holes in the roof or pipes).
- **Blocking Entry Points**: We install barriers, metal strips, and secure seals to make sure your building is "pest-proof."
- **Safe Removal**: We use secure, clean methods to remove pests and prevent them from returning.

**Our Approach**: Your building should be your first line of defense. If we seal the building correctly, the pests stay outside where they belong.

---

## 4. Protecting Your Compound Walls

**The Goal**: For offices, hotels, and homes, we create a strong, clean outer boundary so pests don't even reach your door.

### What We Do:
- **Outer Defense**: We apply regular, safe treatments around the edge of your property to stop crawling insects before they enter.
- **Managing Plants**: We show you how to trim back trees and bushes that touch your walls, which act as "bridges" for pests to get into your house.

**Our Approach**: We treat your perimeter as an active, protective layer, stopping problems before they reach your front door.

---

## How We Work With You

- **Training Your Team**: We can show your local staff how to keep the area clean and well-sealed. This helps you save money and rely less on expensive chemical treatments.
- **Keeping You Safe**: We strictly follow the health guidelines from the Ministry of Health and international standards, so you can trust that our methods are safe for your staff and family.
